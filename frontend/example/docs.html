User Manual

Welcome to the PolyMinder user manual. This guide will help you navigate and utilize the system effectively.

1. Introduction

The PolyMinder system is designed to help users manage and analyze their documents efficiently. This manual will guide you through the various features and functionalities of the system.

2. Features

The PolyMinder system offers the following key features:

2.1 Login and Sign Up

Access to the system requires users to log in or sign up for an account.

How to Log In:

  1. Click the Login button located at the top-right corner of the homepage.
  2. Enter your email and password in the login form.
  3. Click the Log In button to access your account.

How to Sign Up:

  1. Click the Sign Up button located at the top-right corner of the homepage.
  2. Fill in your email, password, and confirm your password in the sign-up form.
  3. Click the Sign Up button to create your account.

2.2 Document Management

Manage your uploaded documents through the user-friendly dashboard.

Accessing the Dashboard:

  1. After logging in, click the Documents button on the header bar.
  2. View your uploaded documents and their processing status in the dashboard.

Dashboard Operations:

  • Upload PDF: Click the Upload Document button to upload new PDF files for processing.
  • View Document: Click on a document title to view its content.
  • Download Original PDF: Download the original PDF file by clicking the download icon.
  • Delete Document: Remove a document from the system by clicking the delete icon.

2.3 Editing Function

After processing, you can verify and edit extracted text, entities, and relationships.

Editing Extracted Text:

  1. Navigate to the Paragraph Viewer by clicking the Paragraph tab on the right sidebar.
  2. Click on highlighted areas in the PDF to view paragraph content.
  3. Click the edit icon to modify the paragraph text.
  4. After editing, click Save & Load to apply changes.

Editing Entities and Relationships:

  1. Go to the Entity Viewer by clicking the Entity tab on the right sidebar.
  2. Click on entities in the PDF or sidebar to view details.
  3. Use the Show All Entities button to highlight all entities in a paragraph.
  4. Click the edit icon to modify an entity.
  5. Change the entity type by selecting from the dropdown menu.
  6. Modify the entity text by selecting new text in the paragraph.
  7. To delete an entity, click the Delete button.
  8. Edit relationships by clicking the Relation tab within the edit window.
  9. Add new relations by clicking the add icon and filling in the details.
  10. After making changes, click Save & Reload to update.

2.4 Filtering Function

Use the filtering function to view specific entities or relationships.

  1. Click on an entity type in the sidebar to filter results.
  2. The PDF viewer will update to show only the selected entities.
  3. For advanced filtering, click the filter icon.
  4. Select the desired relation type and click Apply.

2.5 Download Function

Download your results in various formats for offline use.

  1. Click the Download Result button.
  2. Choose from the available options:
    • All Data
    • Only Confirmed Data
    • Based on Entity Type
    • Based on Current Filter
